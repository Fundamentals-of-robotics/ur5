Both sets of boxes are expected to be in (x1, y1, x2, y2) format.
Arguments:
    box1 (Tensor[N, 4])
    box2 (Tensor[M, 4])
Returns:
    iou (Tensor[N, M]): the NxM matrix containing the pairwise
        IoU values for every element in boxes1 and boxes2
